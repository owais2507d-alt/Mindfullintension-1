Mountain Pose

Description

Mountain Pose (Tadasana) is the foundation for all standing yoga poses. It teaches balance, alignment, and awareness of how you distribute your weight. This pose may look simple, but it engages the entire body and creates a sense of stability and calm.

Do's & Don'ts

Do's
  • • Distribute weight evenly on both feet
  • • Engage your thigh muscles
  • • Lengthen your spine
  • • Relax your shoulders
Don'ts
  • • Lock your knees
  • • Slouch your shoulders
  • • Hold your breath
  • • Tense your face

Benefits

  • Improves posture and body awareness
  • Strengthens thighs, knees, and ankles
  • Relieves sciatica and reduces flat feet
  • Calms the mind and reduces anxiety
